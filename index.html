How your registration is handled

Simple steps so you know what happens after you submit.

Step 1: Submit your form Step 2: We confirm your seat Step 3: Attend your class
  • 1
    Send your details Fill up the registration form on the left. Check that your contact number and email are correct so we can reach you.
  • 2
    Receive confirmation Our team reviews your preferred course and timing. You will get an SMS and email with your confirmed date, time and venue, or we will propose an alternative if your first choice is full.
  • 3
    Prepare your documents Bring your NRIC or FIN, driving licence, and any documents requested in the confirmation message, such as medical forms or SkillsFuture statements.
  • 4
    Attend the course Arrive at least 15 minutes before class starts so you have time to register at the centre and settle in before the session.
